Venue

Kumpula Campus houses the University of Helsinki's Faculty of Science. Workshop venue Exactum is the home for the departments of Computer Science, Mathematics and Seismology. The campus is easily reached by bus and trams. Here you can find the map of the Campus. The city of Helsinki also offers a free web-based Map Service.



Transportation

Using public transportation in Helsinki and capital area is easy. To find a route to your destination, use Journey Planner (Mobile version). There's also a mobile app to buy tickets conveniently.

Hotels in vicinity

If you need accommodation, there are a few hotels near the venue suggested here. You can of course book a hotel further in the city centre: A bus ride from the centre takes approximately half an hour.

Park Hotel Käpylä ☆☆☆

A small, pleasant and affordable hotel in the peaceful district of Käpylä located approximately 2km from campus. Bus stops and a train station located nearby. Small stores and a park in vicinity.

Link: Park Hotel Käpylä

Hotel AVA ☆☆☆

Located on the edge of Kumpula garders and 1.5km from the campus, AVA has easy access to both venue and city centre. Inexpensive

Link: Hotel AVA

Holiday Inn Messukeskus ☆☆☆☆

Located right next to the Helsinki Expo and Convention Centre, this international chained-brand hotel offers everything you'd expect from a bigger hotel for a moderate price. This hotel is located one busride away from Kumpula campus and is also well connected to city centre.

Link: Holiday Inn Messukeskus
